# Mushroom Ceviche

## Ingredients

- A bunch of oyster mushrooms
- 1/2 Red bell pepper
- 1/2 Onion
- Lemon juice
- Cumin, salt, pepper

## Preparation

- Cut the mushrooms, the onion and the bell pepper in small pieces/dices
- Add the lemon juice
- Sason with cumin, salt and pepper
- Let the lemon juice cook the ingredients for at least a night
- Enjoy!
