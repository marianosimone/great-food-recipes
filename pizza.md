# Pizza

Paneer is a fresh cheese common in South Asia, especially in India.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 2 Â½ cups flour
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

- Preheat oven to 450F (230C), and grease a pizza pan/stone.
- Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand for about 10 minutes.
- Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.
- Let rest for at least 5 minutes.
- Shape and cook!
